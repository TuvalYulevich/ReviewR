Notes on the app -

Q: Why the comments and reviews count for the user is not being updated when the app is offline?
A: Because this data relays on real-time and live-data only.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Why the users is not being saved in Room after registration?
A: Because you cannot log into the app when you are offline, so there is no need to save unnecessary data.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Why changes that are made in Firebase do not change directly in Room?
A: These are two different systems, they synchroinse in very specific keypoints in the project (If a change is made from Firebase, it will be updated in the Room as soon the user will see the change in the app itself).
